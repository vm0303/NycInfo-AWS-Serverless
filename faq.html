Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is NYCInfo Tours?
A: NYCInfo Tours is a local-style city tour booking experience. We help visitors explore NYC beyond the typical tourist checklist—neighborhoods, stories, and routes that feel authentic and easy to follow.
Q: What kinds of tours do you offer?
A: We offer a mix of neighborhood walks, landmark highlights, and themed routes (food, culture, photo spots). Each tour includes a clear meeting point, estimated duration, and route overview so you can plan confidently.
Q: How do I book a tour?
A: Go to the “Book A Tour With Us!” page, pick your package, choose a date/time, and submit your details. You’ll see a confirmation message once your request is received.
Q: Where do tours start?
A: Every tour has a specific meeting point listed in the booking flow. We choose spots that are easy to find (major corners, station exits, or well-known entrances) so you’re never guessing.
Q: How long are the tours?
A: Tour length depends on the package and the pace of the group. Most tours range from about 30 minutes to 2 hours and 30 minutes. You’ll see the estimated duration before you book.
Q: What should I bring?
A: Comfortable walking shoes, weather-appropriate layers, and a water bottle. If it’s a photo-heavy route, bring your phone or camera fully charged.
Q: Are the tours family-friendly?
A: Yes. Many tours work great for families. If you’re booking with kids, choose a route with shorter walking stretches and let us know in the booking notes.
Q: Are tours accessible?
A: We aim to make routes practical and welcoming. Accessibility varies by neighborhood (curbs, stairs, subway access), so add a note when booking and we’ll guide you to the best option.
Q: What happens if it rains or the weather is bad?
A: Light rain usually doesn’t stop a tour—NYC is still NYC. For severe weather, we’ll recommend rescheduling or adjusting the route to keep it safe and enjoyable.
Q: Can I change or cancel my booking?
A: If you need to change your time or cancel, reach out as early as possible. We’ll do our best to accommodate schedule changes based on availability.
Q: Do you offer private tours?
A: Yes—private tours are great for couples, families, and small groups. Use the booking page and check mark “private tour” so we can tailor the pacing and highlights.
