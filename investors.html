Who are we?

Built by locals. Backed by builders.

We’re NYC locals who love building great experiences—both on the street and on the web. NYCInfo Tours is a city-tour booking experience inspired by real itineraries. Our mission is to help visitors explore NYC like insiders with tours that are welcoming, well-paced, and packed with stories. And behind the scenes, we build a modern booking flow that’s fast, reliable, and simple to use.

What we believe

Three principles that shape every route and every click.

Local-first

Explore like you belong here

Our tours are built by New Yorkers who know the rhythm of the city. That means real neighborhoods, meaningful stories, and places you won’t find on a standard tourist map.

Guest-ready

Comfort over confusion

From meeting spots to pacing, everything is designed to feel effortless. You can relax and enjoy the city without worrying about logistics or timing.

Book with confidence

No surprises, just great tours

Clear descriptions, honest durations, and transparent options help you choose exactly what fits your day and travel style.

Our commitment

Local collaborations that keep tours authentic.

Neighborhood Partner Network

Local small-business collaboration

We team up with independent cafes, galleries, and venues across Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens to surface experiences you won't find in generic guidebooks. These partnerships help us keep tours authentic, support local business, and offer perks like priority entry or add-on tastings on select routes.

Local Guide Collective

Real NYC insight, not tourist scripts

Our routes are shaped by New Yorkers who actually spend time in these neighborhoods—so you get the spots, stories, and pacing that feel authentic. Expect clear meeting points, smooth flow, and a tour that’s easy to follow but never generic.

Community & Safety Partners

Accessibility and guest experience

We design tours that are welcoming and practical: clear meeting points, realistic pacing, and options for different mobility needs. We continuously improve based on guest feedback, and we publish easy-to-follow guidance so visitors can feel confident exploring NYC.
